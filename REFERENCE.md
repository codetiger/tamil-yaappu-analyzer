# References

1. **Project Madurai**
   - An open, voluntary, worldwide initiative devoted to the preparation of electronic texts of Tamil literary works and their free distribution on the Internet. We are deeply grateful to Project Madurai and its volunteers for their decades-long dedication to preserving and making accessible the rich heritage of Tamil literature in digital form.
   - Source for classical Tamil verse texts used in the test corpus:
     - **Naladiyar** (நாலடியார்) — 400 verses, used for `nerisai_venba` and `innisai_venba` test data
     - **Nalavenba** (நளவெண்பா) — 372 verses, used for `nerisai_venba` test data
     - **Kurunthokai** (குறுந்தொகை) — 401 Sangam-era poems, used for `nerisai_asiriyappa`, `nilaimandila_asiriyappa`, and other pa-type test data
   - URL: https://www.projectmadurai.org

2. **Thirukkural Alagiduthal and Kural Venba Validation using Python**
   - Authors: Thivaharan S, Gayathri C, Vijay Raj Kumar M
   - Journal: Journal of Trends in Computer Science and Smart Technology (ISSN: 2582-4104), Vol. 5, Issue 1, Pages 51–66, March 2023
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.36548/jtcsst.2023.1.004

3. **Thirukkural.io**
   - Source for all 1330 Thirukurals used in the corpus (`kural.json`)
   - URL: https://thirukkural.io
